Research & Innovation Wing

In partnership with EhealthyInfo and the American Journal of Patient Health Info (AJPHI)

At USMLEInkspire, we believe research is not just an academic exercise—it's a gateway to deeper understanding, stronger CVs, powerful connections, and real-world impact. That’s why our premium packages offer an exclusive 3-month Research & Innovation Internship in collaboration with our esteemed research partners: EhealthyInfo and AJPHI.

What You’ll Gain: 3-Month Research & Innovation Internship

Package customers will be granted access to our structured, mentored internship where they become active contributors to the Research & Innovation Wing of EhealthyInfo and AJPHI. This immersive experience is tailored to enhance both your ERAS application and your long-term academic and professional profile.

Core Opportunities

Active Research Participation

  • Join ongoing research projects led by experienced mentors.
  • Design original studies, write protocols, and collect & interpret data.
  • Engage in systematic reviews and meta-analyses.

Biostatistics & Database Workshops

  • Training in statistical tools and evidence synthesis platforms.
  • Support on literature search, data abstraction, and critical appraisal.

Scholarly Writing, Reviewing & Editing

  • Co-author articles, reports, or reviews for AJPHI and affiliated platforms.
  • Training in manuscript structuring and citation formatting.
  • Editorial experience under supervision.

One-on-One Mentorship

  • Personalized mentorship by accomplished physician-scientists and editors.
  • Mentor matching based on goals and progress tracking.

Professional Advancement Pathways

  • Eligible interns may be promoted to Research Assistant roles.
  • Top performers may join the Editorial Board as Student or Academic Editors.

Certificate of Completion

Officially endorsed by EhealthyInfo and AJPHI. Can be listed under “Research Experience,” “Volunteer Work,” or “Publications” on your ERAS CV.

Presentation & Networking Opportunities

  • Support in submitting abstracts or posters to conferences.
  • Opportunities to publish and network globally.

Our Research Partners

EhealthyInfo

EhealthyInfo is a healthcare education platform founded by a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals. Their mission is to simplify complex medical information and empower patients through accessible and reliable health education content.

American Journal of Patient Health Info (AJPHI)

AJPHI is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to making health research understandable and actionable for the public. Articles focus on common illnesses, prevention, and public health awareness. AJPHI serves as an academic platform for our internship participants.

Why This Matters

Research and academic involvement are increasingly valued across specialties. This internship builds your scholarly identity and provides rich experiences for your application, personal statement, and interviews.

How to Join

This opportunity is exclusively available to enrollees in the following USMLEInkspire packages:

  • IMG Success Package
  • IMG Comprehensive Package

If you're ready to elevate your profile and make meaningful contributions to medical education and research, this internship is for you.
