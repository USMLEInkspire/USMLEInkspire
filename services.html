Our Services

I. ERAS Application Strategy Session

Many IMGs and USMGs struggle with the overwhelming number of residency programs, visa policies, and regional competitiveness. This personalized session offers you a roadmap tailored to your unique strengths and goals.

  • Program Selection Guidance: Factors like research focus, location, IMG friendliness, and visa preferences.
  • Specialty Competitiveness Insights: Realistic evaluation of your chances.
  • Residency Roadmap: Action plan with a timeline.
  • Interactive Q&A: Discuss concerns live with a mentor.
  • Mentor prep using your pre-call questionnaire and program databases.
View Packages

II. CV & Personal Statement (PS) Review

First impressions matter. Our CV & PS reviews transform your documents into compelling narratives that reflect your identity and readiness for U.S. residency training.

  • Grooming Package – Group Session: Small groups (3–5) for guided feedback, ERAS formatting, grammar correction, and peer learning.
  • Grooming Package – Individual Session: One-on-one support for draft improvement, with mentor-guided reviews and margin comments.
  • Comprehensive Review: Line-by-line editing, storytelling enhancement, program-specific tailoring, and a revision round with mentor feedback.
  • Mentor Guidance: Structural critique, storytelling elevation, and formatting refinement.
View Packages

III. Mock Interviews

Interviews test more than knowledge—they evaluate confidence, communication, and cultural awareness. Our sessions help you master the art of presenting your best self.

  • Individual Session: Realistic 45-min mock interview followed by 15-min personalized feedback with actionable tips.
  • 3-Session Package: Includes all individual benefits, plus progressive improvement tracking and tailored specialty feedback.
  • Mentor Strategy: Mix of behavioral, ethical, and scenario-based questions tailored to your field; verbal and non-verbal evaluation; written summary with improvements.
View Packages

IV. Step 3 CCS Strategy Session

The Computer-based Case Simulations (CCS) of Step 3 are crucial for H1B visa candidates. This session equips you with high-yield strategies to confidently manage simulated patient encounters.

  • Simulation Walkthrough: 60-minute live guidance on test format, time management, and decision flow.
  • Software Tips: Guidance on using CCS practice tools effectively.
  • High-Yield Cases: Key differentials, actions, and common errors to avoid.
  • Post-Session Deliverables: CCS Tipsheet PDF and follow-up Q&A access.
View Packages

V. ERAS Research Section Editing

Your research efforts deserve more than a generic entry. We refine your ERAS research section to reflect clarity, structure, and scholarly contribution—without exceeding character limits.

  • Concise, structured rewriting using active voice.
  • Clear role and contribution statements for publications, abstracts, and posters.
  • Impact-oriented highlights to demonstrate academic potential.
  • Improves appeal for research-focused programs.
